About SnapSave

SnapSave is a free online video downloader that helps you save videos from YouTube and Instagram with just one click. Our mission is to provide a simple, fast, and secure way to download your favorite videos for offline viewing.

Founded in 2023, SnapSave has helped millions of users worldwide access their favorite content anytime, anywhere. Our tool requires no registration and works directly in your browser.

Disclaimer

SnapSave is designed for downloading videos for personal use only. Please respect copyright laws and only download videos you have permission to use or that are in the public domain.

We do not host any videos on our servers. All videos are downloaded directly from their original sources. SnapSave is not responsible for how users utilize downloaded content.

Privacy Policy

At SnapSave, we take your privacy seriously. We do not collect or store any personal information when you use our service. Video URLs you submit for downloading are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers.

We may use cookies to improve your experience on our website. These cookies do not contain personal information and are used solely for analytics and functionality purposes.
